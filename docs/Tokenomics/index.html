Tokenomics 📊

Tokenomics Pie chart and table come here

Total Supply: 1 Billion Tokens 💰​

  • Public Sale: 20% (200 Million Tokens) 🚀
    Offered to the public to raise funds for platform development, marketing, and operations.

  • Liquidity Pool: 10% (100 Million Tokens) 💧
    A liquidity pool is a collection of funds locked in a smart contract on a decentralized finance (DeFi) platform.

  • Team & Founders: 15% (150 Million Tokens) 👥
    Allocated to the founding team, developers, and advisors. Subject to a vesting period of 2 years with a 30 months cliff to ensure long-term commitment.

  • Ecosystem and Community Incentives: 10% (100 Million Tokens) 🌱
    Reserved for liquidity mining, user rewards, community challenges, and other incentives to encourage participation and growth of the ecosystem.

  • Marketing: 10% (100 million Tokens) 📢
    Used to broaden our reach and strengthen our ecosystem via strategic alliances and marketing initiatives.

  • Partnerships and Strategic Alliances: 5% (50 Million Tokens) 🤝
    Set aside for future partnerships, collaborations, and strategic initiatives that align with BRCGate's mission and expansion plans.

  • Advisors: 5% (50 Million Tokens) 🧠
    Set aside for future advisors and strategic initiatives that align with BRCGate's mission and expansion plans.

  • Staking Rewards: 10% (100 Million Tokens) 🏆
    Allocated for distribution as staking rewards to users who participate in network security, governance, or provide liquidity.

  • Reserve Fund: 10% (100 Million Tokens) 🛡️
    A contingency fund to provide flexibility for unforeseen expenses, market interventions, and strategic investments to support the platform's longevity.

  • Development Fund: 5% (50 Million Tokens) 💻
    Dedicated to funding ongoing development, technical upgrades, and new features on the BRCGate platform.

Deflationary Mechanisms 🔥​

  • Transaction Fee Burns: 0.5% of all transaction fees collected in BRCGate tokens will be burned, permanently reducing the total supply.
  • Quarterly Buyback and Burn: Up to 2% of the platform's quarterly profits will be used to buy back and burn BRCGate tokens.

Governance 🗳️​

Token holders will have governance rights proportional to their holdings, enabling them to vote on key decisions, development proposals, and changes to the tokenomics model.
